Frequently Asked Questions:
Opening hours and reservations

What are the opening hours of your restaurant?
Our restaurant is open from 12:00 to 23:00, every day.

What is the cost of booking a table?
Table reservation is free. You can book a table by phone or online.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, Visa, Mastercard.

Services

Do you have Wi-Fi?
Yes, we have free Wi-Fi for our guests.

Do you have parking?
Yes, we have free parking on the restaurant grounds.

Do you have other services?
Yes, we have food delivery at home, and we also hold banquets and other events.

Menu

Do you have a children's menu?
We have a special children's menu with dishes suitable for children of all ages.

Do you have a menu for vegetarians and vegans?
Yes, we have a menu for vegetarians and vegans, which you can get from the waiter.

What desserts do you have?
We have delicious desserts including cakes, pastries, ice cream, and fruits.

Casarecce

A small-sized paste, similar to small rolled-up scrolls. Previously, this was done by twisting rectangles of dough around thin wooden pins or a metal rod.

Farfalle

TOne of the oldest types of pasta, which appeared in the 16th century in Lombardy and Emilia-Romagna.

Fettuccine

Pasta with a delicate taste, a very favorite in Rome. It differs from tagliatelle and pappardelle only in width, and here size matters. One of the most popular fettuccine dishes is fettuccine alfredo.
